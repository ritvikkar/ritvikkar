FAQs

Where am I from?

Short Answer: Dehradun, India.
Long Answer: I was born in Dehradun, India so that's my fall back to the "Where are you from?" question! My family lived in a small town called Ganeshpuri, Maharashtra till I was about 6.
We then shifted to Tiruchirappalli (Trichy), Tamil Nadu for about a year and moved out of India to Nigeria. I lived in Nigeria for 6 years with my family and went back to India to attend The Doon School in 2010.
From 2010 to 2015 I was a student at Doon, traveling twice a year back to my family in Nigeria. In 2013, my parents moved to Fujairah, UAE our current home. It's hard for me to identify Fujairah as the place I'm from since I barely spend a month or two a year there. My family is there, so in that sense Fujairah is my home but for a place to be home it takes a lot more than that.
I'd say at this moment in my life, I'm from Northfield, MN where I attend Carleton College. Northfield is where most of my year is spent and it's where my life's been establised for the past 3 years!

Why Product Management?

For the longest time, I felt burdened by my professional life. I started studing Computer Science in 2013 and have been studying it since. I'd always assumed that one day I'd go on to work as a developer at a tech company but that wasn't really ambition or passion that was leading me there.
In 2016 an a Scholars in Technology program in San Fransisco, I learnt about the Product part of the tech industry. I was at Facebook, and a Carleton alum introduced himself and described what he did for a living. I remember thinking, "That's it. That's what I want to be." And for the months following the program, I reached out to a number of Product Managers to learn about the field and see if it was meant for me.
I ended up interning at Vevo as a Product Manager lasr summer and that opportunity was literally everything that gets me excited and more, packed in one job.
